Welcome to Our Website

Thank you for visiting our website! We are dedicated to providing assistance to individuals with visual impairments. Our mission is to enhance accessibility and independence for people who are blind or visually impaired.

By leveraging innovative technology, we offer solutions that help users navigate and interact with the digital world more effectively. Our website provides tools and resources designed to empower individuals with visual impairments to access information and communicate

Explore our website to discover how our services can benefit you or someone you care about. Click on the logo at the center to navigate to the Home page and begin your journey towards a more accessible online experience.

On this home page, you can click on the 'Login' or 'Register' button to access all the website's features for free.

Here, you can use the demo login button for now. We don't have authentication services available yet; it's just the prototype of our project to demonstrate what we can do in the future.

After logging in, you will be directed to the website. Here, you can utilize the 'Live Feed' button to navigate to the monitoring page.

The live feed from the blind person's camera, along with the object detection output, will appear inside the red rectangle here.

You can utilize the provided features, such as the Map to determine his exact location. The weather icon will provide comprehensive information about the weather, and we also offer an AQI detector. Additionally, you can switch between light and dark mode as desired.
